Treatments

DENTAL IMPLANT

In our dental practice in Bogenhausen, Munich, we offer you a wide range of implant sizes and variants, which enable an optimal restoration of the natural dentition.

ROOT CANAL TREATMENT

After a deep carious lesion invasion of a tooth or even an accident, an inflammation of the dental nerves can occur. The only way to preserve the diseased tooth is root canal treatment, also known as an endodontic treatment.

PROFESSIONAL CLEANING

Professional tooth cleaning (PZR) is a main component of dental prophylaxis. It is a comprehensive mechanical cleaning of the teeth, which eliminates the deficits in daily oral hygiene.

AESTHETICAL DENTISTRY

Aesthetic problems and defects of the teeth often merge seamlessly. A chipped edge or an old filling not only looks unsightly, but also makes the tooth particularly susceptible to further damage. With the help of e.g. veeners or inlays. the teeth can be restored "invisibly".

ORTHODONTICS

Invisalign - The invisible braces.

CHILDREN DENTISTRY

Paediatric dentistry includes all treatments to maintain healthy teeth in children and adolescents.
